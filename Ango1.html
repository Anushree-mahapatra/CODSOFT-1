About Us

HelpingHands is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of the underprivileged. We believe that small steps can create big changes. Our mission is to provide education, healthcare, and empowerment to those in need.

Healthcare Camps

We organize free medical camps and awareness drives regularly.

Women Empowerment

We support women through skill-building and self-help programs.
